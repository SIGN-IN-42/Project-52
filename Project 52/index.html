Steps to make a flower

Step1: Take a paper of desired colour and fold it as shown in figure below. Make 4 such.

Step2: Draw and cut the curve as shown and cut a little part of the ending.

Step3: Open the folded paper and then from the 1st one, cut only one part and from the 2nd, 2 parts and follow for the rest.

Step4: Separate it and glue them together as shown in the picture below.

Step5: After that pile them up.

Step6: Glue them all together.

Step7: Add detailing as desired.
